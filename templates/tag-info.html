CitizensForCommunity


Tag Information:
Tags in the CitizensForCommunity web application is used to allow users to refine their search and accurately classify the issue they're reporting, essentially making it easier for the right people to use the right resources to resolve the problem at hand.
Vandalism:
Action involving deliberate destruction of or damage to public or private property.
Unsanitary:
Anything that can potentially pose a risk to the public's health.
Excessive Litter:
Excessive trash that is left lying in open or public places.
Landscaping Issue(s):
Issues with foliage or other landscaping items in public areas.
Pollution:
The presence in or introduction into the environment of a substance or thing that has harmful or poisonous effects.
Structural Integrity:
Health of building support systems.
Transportation Infrastructure:
Health of any public infrastructure including roads and bridges.
Miscellaneous Maintenance:
Any other instances where public interference or maintenance is required.
