Tag Information:
Tags in the CitizensForCommunity web application is used to allow users to refine their search and accurately classify the issue they're reporting, essentially making it easier for the right people to use the right resources to resolve the problem at hand.
Vandalism:
Action involving deliberate destruction of or damage to public or private property.
Illegal Drug Use:
Use of any illegal drugs. (Check with local authorities on which drugs are illegal in your area)
Loitering:
Standing around or waiting in one area with no clear purpose. (Check local loitering laws to determine where it is illegal to loiter in your area)
Underage Substance Use:
Consumption of drugs that are age-restricted by those who are not yet at the correct age limit. (Check local laws to find out which drugs are age-restricted and the drugs specific age restrictions)
Public Indecency:
The deliberate exposure in public or in view of the general public by a person of a portion or portions of their body in circumstances where the exposure is contrary to local moral or other standards of appropriate behavior.
Unsanitary:
Anything that can potentially pose a risk to the public's health.
Excessive Litter:
Excessive trash that is left lying in open or public places.
Excessive Bodily Fluids:
Excessive presence of bodily fluids in a public area.
Landscaping Issue(s):
Issues with foliage in public areas.
Pollution:
The presence in or introduction into the environment of a substance or thing that has harmful or poisonous effects.
Environmental Safety:
Anything that can potentially pose a risk to the health of the environment.
